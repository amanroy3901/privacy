Privacy Policy for 2048 Neon Rush

Last Updated: May 06, 2025

Welcome to the electrifying world of 2048 Neon Rush! We're a puzzle game crafted with love by Aman Kumar, and we’re here to light up your gaming experience—while keeping your privacy safe. Let’s dive into how we handle your data with transparency and care.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

2048 Neon Rush is all about fun without the fuss. Designed as a lightweight, offline game, we keep data collection to the bare minimum to ensure your privacy:

  • Personal Information: We don’t collect any personal details like your name, email, or phone number—unless you choose to share them with us (like when reaching out for support).
  • Non-Personal Information:
    • Game Data: Your high score and grid size preferences are saved locally on your device. This keeps your progress ready for your next neon-filled session, and it never leaves your device!
    • Device Information: We might gather anonymized details, like your device type or OS version, to make sure the game runs smoothly. Don’t worry—this isn’t tied to your identity.
    • Usage Data: We collect anonymized stats (think session length or grid sizes you play) to help us improve the game. It’s all aggregated, so you stay completely anonymous.

We steer clear of sensitive data like your location, contacts, or financial info—your privacy is our top priority!

2. How Do We Use Your Information?

The little data we collect is all about making 2048 Neon Rush better for you:

  • Game Functionality: We store your high score and grid preferences locally to keep your progress safe between games.
  • App Improvement: Anonymized device and usage data helps us squash bugs, boost performance, and dream up cool new features.
  • Support: If you reach out to us, we’ll use any info you share (like your email) to help solve your queries with a neon glow of care.

We don’t use your data for ads, marketing, or anything unrelated to making your gaming experience awesome.

3. Do We Share Your Information?

We’re not in the business of sharing, selling, or disclosing your data—except in these rare cases:

  • Legal Obligations: If the law requires it (like a court order), we may need to share data.
  • Service Providers: We might use third-party tools (like crash reporting services) that process anonymized data to help us improve the app. These partners are bound by strict confidentiality rules.

Rest assured, your high scores and game data stay on your device—they don’t get sent to us or anyone else.

4. How Do We Keep Your Data Safe?

We take security seriously to protect the data we collect:

  • Local Storage: Your game data (like high scores) lives on your device and doesn’t travel to our servers.
  • Security Practices: Any anonymized data we collect (like usage stats) is encrypted during transmission and stored securely.

While we do our best, no system is 100% secure. If you’re worried about your data, just let us know—we’re here to help!

5. A Safe Space for Kids

2048 Neon Rush is a game for all ages, including kids under 13. We follow the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and other laws to keep things safe:

  • We don’t knowingly collect personal info from children under 13.
  • Our app skips in-app purchases, ads, or social features that might accidentally collect kids’ data.
  • If we find out a child under 13 has shared personal info (like through a support email), we’ll delete it right away.

Parents, if you have any concerns about your child’s experience with the app, please reach out—we’d love to help!

6. Your Rights, Your Control

You’re in charge of your data with 2048 Neon Rush. Here’s how you can manage it:

  • Access and Deletion: Since your game data is stored locally, you can reset your high score or preferences by clearing the app’s data in your device settings.
  • Opt-Out: We don’t use your data for marketing, so there’s nothing to opt out of.
  • Uninstall: If you uninstall the app, all your locally stored game data gets removed from your device.

7. No Third-Party Links Here

2048 Neon Rush keeps your experience pure—no third-party links, ads, or external content to pull you away. It’s just you, the glowing tiles, and the puzzle fun!

8. Updates to Our Privacy Policy

We might tweak this policy as our practices or laws change. You’ll find the updated version right here, with a new “Last Updated” date. If we make big changes, we’ll let you know in the app. Check back now and then to stay in the loop!
