Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. 2048 Neon Rush is designed to provide a fun, secure and mostly offline gaming experience.

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

Overview

2048 Neon Rush is a puzzle game that works primarily offline. Most features can be enjoyed without creating an account. Google Sign-In is completely optional and is only used for cloud save features.

Information We Collect

  • Guest Players: High scores, theme preferences, settings and game progress stored locally on your device.
  • Google Sign-In Users: Google display name, account identifier and cloud save data such as scores and preferences.

How We Use Information

  • Save and restore game progress.
  • Store high scores.
  • Remember selected themes and settings.
  • Synchronize progress across devices.
  • Provide optional cloud save functionality.

Data Storage

If you play as a guest, your data remains on your device.


If you choose to sign in with Google, selected game data may be securely stored using Google's services to support cloud saves and synchronization.

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Precise location information.
  • Contacts.
  • Photos or videos.
  • Messages.
  • Financial or payment information.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Sign-In
  • Google Play Services
  • Google Play Games Services (if enabled)

These services operate under their own privacy policies and terms.

Your Choices

  • Play without signing in.
  • Sign out of Google at any time.
  • Clear app data through device settings.
  • Uninstall the application.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a revised update date.
